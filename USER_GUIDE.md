# 🪙 SHx Tip Bot - User Testing Guide

Welcome to the SHx Tip Bot! This bot allows you to instantly send and receive SHx tokens directly inside Discord.

Here is a quick guide on how to test its features:

## 1. Receiving Tips (Instant & Free!)
You don't need to set up *anything* to receive SHx! If someone tips you or you claim an `/airdrop`, the bot instantly creates an internal secure ledger for you. 
> 💡 *Note: Because this is a testing environment simulating fake SHx, the Admin will initiate your testing journey by tipping you your first batch of test tokens!*
- **Check your funds**: Type `/balance` at any time to see your current holdings!

## 2. Sending Tips
You can tip anyone in the server instantly, with zero network fees or wait times. 
- **Command**: `/tip @username 10 Thanks for the help!`
- The bot seamlessly deducts 10 SHx (plus a tiny 1 SHx bot fee) from your `/balance` and instantly credits it to their balance. 

## 3. Linking Your Stellar Wallet
When you are ready to pull your SHx out of Discord and into your personal Stellar wallet, you need to verify your address.
- **Command**: `/link`
- The bot will DM you a secure, private 15-minute link. 
- Click the link to open the web dashboard and connect your Stellar wallet (like Freighter or Lobstr).

## 4. Withdrawing to the Blockchain
Once your wallet is linked, you can withdraw your internal SHx to the real Stellar Network!
- **Command**: `/withdraw 50 GABCDEFGHIJKLMNOP...`
- The bot will process your request and give you a real, live Stellar transaction hash proving the SHx arrived in your wallet!

*Enjoy lightning-fast tipping! Let us know if you find any bugs!*
